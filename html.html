00 — Problem

Uncontrolled violations make Indian intersections chaotic and unsafe.

Manual policing at dense junctions is slow, biased and hard to scale. CCTV footage often remains a passive recording tool instead of becoming an active enforcement system.

Junction Chaos Snapshot
  • Multiple bikes share the wrong lane to skip signal waiting time.
  • Majority of riders skip helmets on short-distance trips.
  • CCTV cameras exist but lack an intelligence layer to act on violations in real time.
Objective Turn passive cameras into active AI enforcers.

01 — Solution

AI that sees, understands, and acts on traffic violations.

We integrate YOLO-based object detection with a smart violation engine that understands lane rules, helmets, and signal timing to trigger automated alerts and evidence recording.

Stage 01
Video Ingestion

Live CCTV or uploaded junction footage is fed to the system using a web interface.

Stage 02
YOLO Detection

Vehicles, lanes, and helmets are detected frame-by-frame using an optimized YOLO model.

Stage 03
Rule Engine

Logical rules verify if vehicles are in wrong lanes, skipping helmets, or breaking signal states.

Stage 04
Violation Actions

Violations trigger frame capture, metadata logging, and optional e-mail dispatch to authorities.

02 — System Architecture

Fully modular, from camera stream to violation report.

The architecture is designed to be plug-and-play with existing CCTV setups, supporting both live RTSP feeds and offline recorded videos.

  • • Ingestion Layer: Video capture & basic pre-processing.
  • • AI Layer: YOLO detection, lane mapping, helmet classification.
  • • Intelligence Layer: Violation logic, event tracking.
  • • Reporting Layer: Snapshot capture, JSON logs, optional mail integration.
